Who am I?


Christophe Servan is AI Principal Scientist at Intescia since 2024 and part-time lecturer at Polytech'Saclay since 2024. He is part of the head of ATALA, the French National association for NLP since 2020.

His main interest is in multilingual Natural Language Processing jointly with Deep Learning for Information Retrieval.

I focus on modeling context for several fields of NLP: Spoken Language Understanding, semantic content analysis and modeling, machine translation, Intent Detection in real-life application at scale.

Since 2022, he collaborate as Researcher with the LISN-CNRS lab part of the LIPS team
